Change your Password

To change your password:

  • Enter your username and current password in the corresponding fields.
  • Enter a OTP token only if 2FA is enabled on your account.
  • Enter your desired password, and then confirm it, before clicking Reset.
  • You can then log into the PORT.DIRECT web services by clicking Login.
